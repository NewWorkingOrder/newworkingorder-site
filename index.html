Why AMS

Experience first. Technology where it belongs.

Most industrial problems do not start with software. They start in the quote, the service delay, the missed detail, the handoff, the unclear ownership, or the decision that keeps getting pushed because the information is weak.

AMS starts there first. The work begins with people, process, and operating reality, then builds the right level of support around it. Many of the problems are not about effort. They are about not having the right tools in the right places.
