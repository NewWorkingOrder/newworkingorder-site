Why AMS

Decision quality before software theater.

Most industrial problems do not begin as software problems. They begin where information degrades, where handoffs get loose, where quoting slows down, where ownership gets blurry, or where the customer is being asked to make a high-consequence decision with weak context.

AMS starts with expertise and discovery, then builds the right level of intervention around the work itself: process design, focused workflow apps, departmental intelligence, and local architecture only when it actually fits the operation.
