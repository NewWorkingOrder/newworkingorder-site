1. Information We Collect

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how we protect it, and the choices you may have regarding your information.

Information you provide directly. When you contact us, submit a form, request information, or communicate with us, we may collect your name, company name, email address, phone number, job title, location, details about your business, equipment, processes, or inquiry, and any other information you choose to provide.

Information collected automatically. When you visit our website, we may automatically collect certain technical and usage information such as IP address, browser type, device type, operating system, pages viewed, time spent on pages, referral source, and general website interaction data.

2. How We Use Information

We may use collected information to:

  • Respond to inquiries and requests.
  • Provide information about our services.
  • Improve our website, services, and user experience.
  • Communicate with you about your inquiry or relationship with us.
  • Analyze website traffic and usage trends.
  • Maintain security and protect against fraud or misuse.
  • Support internal business operations.
  • Develop, improve, or support workflow, automation, and AI-enabled business tools used in connection with our services.

3. Cookies and Analytics

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to improve functionality, understand user behavior, and measure website performance. We may use analytics providers such as Google Analytics or similar services to help us understand how visitors use our website. You can usually control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect certain website functions.

4. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only in limited circumstances such as the following:

  • With service providers who help us operate our website or business.
  • With software, hosting, analytics, communication, CRM, or workflow providers supporting our operations.
  • With AI-enabled service providers or tools used to support communication, intake, workflow, or operational processes.
  • When required by law, legal process, or regulatory request.
  • To protect our rights, property, operations, or safety, or those of others.
  • In connection with a business transfer, merger, sale, or reorganization.

Any sharing is limited to what is reasonably necessary for business operations or legal compliance.

5. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to respond to inquiries, maintain business records, support services and operations, comply with legal, tax, insurance, or regulatory obligations, and improve our processes and systems. We may retain certain information longer when needed for legitimate business purposes or legal compliance.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect information from unauthorized access, disclosure, misuse, or loss. However, no website, transmission method, or storage system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Third-Party Links and Services

Our website may contain links to third-party websites, tools, or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those third parties. We encourage you to review their privacy policies separately.

8. Your Choices

You may choose not to provide certain information, but that may limit our ability to respond or provide services. If you would like to request access to, correction of, or deletion of information you have submitted to us, please contact us using the information below. We will review and respond as appropriate under applicable law.

9. Children’s Privacy

Our website and services are intended for business and professional audiences and are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
